About me

Kundan Kumar is an accomplished Data Scientist specializing in AI-Driven Financial Data Analytics, with expertise in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning across various domains. He possesses extensive experience in time series data analysis and medical image analysis. Currently, he is dedicated to crafting automated trading systems using advanced machine learning algorithms at Data-Core Systems (India) Pvt. Ltd. Previously, he spent five years as an Associate Professor in academia.

Passionate about simplifying intricate data, especially in finance and healthcare, to yield impactful results, Kundan brings a unique combination of academic rigor and practical industry acumen to the forefront of cutting-edge data science applications.

Always eager to expand his knowledge base, Kundan seeks challenging roles that prioritize innovation, emphasizing machine learning, time-series data analysis, and statistical data mining. He is enthusiastic about contributing his expertise to a dynamic team, driving data-driven decision-making and optimizing strategies to propel the company's success.
